Frequently Asked Questions

What does a car accident lawyer do?

A car accident lawyer specializes in providing legal representation to individuals involved in car accidents. They help clients navigate insurance claims, negotiate settlements, and pursue lawsuits if necessary.

Do I need a car accident lawyer if I have insurance?

Even if you have insurance, a lawyer can help ensure you receive fair compensation and navigate any disputes with the insurance company. They can also assist if the other party is uninsured or underinsured.

What if the other driver doesn't have insurance?

If the other driver is uninsured or underinsured, you may still have options for compensation. Your own insurance policy may include uninsured/underinsured motorist coverage, or you may pursue a lawsuit against the at-fault driver.
